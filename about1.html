WHAT IS CUPS?

Clubs United For A Progressive Strategy is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization for young adults aged 15-25 that serve specific needs of youth in the areas of civis education, youth and college internship identification, mentoring, tutoring, community wellness organizing and a variety of needs for the youth of Davidson County and the Middle Tennessee area.
