WHAT IS C.U.P.S?

CUPS is a youth based organizationed (ages 15-25) that serves specific needs of youth in the area of civics education. Youth and college internship identification, mentoring, tutoring, community wellness organizing, and a variety of needs for the youth of Davidson County and the Middle Tennessee area.

Clubs United For A Progressive Strategy is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization for young adults aged 15-25 that serve specific needs of youth in the areas of civis education, youth and college internship identification, mentoring, tutoring, community wellness organizing and a variety of needs for the youth of Davidson County and the Middle Tennessee area.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to preserve, conserve and restore civic engagement, education and to enhance the quality of life for young adults, college students, young professionals and communities through ethical and moral choices, volunteerism, and activities.
